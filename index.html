Turkmenistan, is a neutral and independent country in Central Asia. It has a long history, culture, traditions and attractions.

Excerpts from Lonely Planet

…it's an ancient land of great spirituality, tradition and natural beauty.
“The ancient cities of Merv and Konye-Urgench inspire visions of caravans plodding along the ancient Silk Road, while the haunting beauty of the Karakum desert and other quirky natural phenomena are equally mesmerising. The full Turkmen experience is ultimately about mingling with the warm and fascinating people themselves, whose hospitality is the stuff of legend.”

Why another site?

Why another site? Nowadays,if somebody has a desire to make a trip, she/he can easily find some information by googling. And we all know where to start. We read on Lonely Planet, Trip Advisor, travel operators and agencies sites and other platforms like Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, etc. Some would read books or speak with colleagues who has been there. And it’s all fine and normal.

After some time, we learn about historical, archeological, cultural and natural attractions of Turkmenistan. However, the information is widely dispersed and many people remain puzzled. To tackle this issue, we created this consolidating travel blog to reduce searching time, offer more specialized information and stories
